FAQ

Common questions

Straight answers on the working check, efficiency, compare and pricing.

What do I receive in a Sunsynk Report?

A working check and System Performance Score, efficiency reviews with prioritised suggestions, peer compare against neighbouring systems, monetary insight on grid import, and an installer-ready PDF based on roughly one month of Sunsynk readings.

Is there a subscription?

No. Bundles are a fixed cost for report credits that never expire. Starter, Classic, and Pro start from £2.99, R69.99, or $4.29 depending on currency. There is no subscription.

Who is the report for?

Homeowners use it to confirm the system is working, see if it is efficient, and understand the bill impact. Installers and consultants use it for standardised documentation and recommendations backed by peer compare.

How does report generation work?

Connect your Sunsynk system, analyse about one month of readings, receive the working check, efficiency suggestions and cost insight, then download the PDF. Purchase and generation happen at app.sunsynkreports.com.
