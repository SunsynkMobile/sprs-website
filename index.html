What should I do next?

Prioritised actions, not a pile of charts

Findings consolidate into a ranked plan: behavioural changes, configuration improvements and hardware upgrades.

Behaviour

  • Shift heavy evening loads earlier while solar or battery cover is available
  • Review habit patterns that drive peak grid import

Configuration

  • Adjust battery reserve and discharge timing for evening peaks
  • Tighten export and self-use settings before buying more capacity

Hardware

  • Consider storage or PV only after clipping and scheduling are reviewed
  • Share the PDF with your installer when an upgrade case is clear

Assumptions, measured versus estimated values, analysis scope and disclaimers appear in Important Information at the end of every report.

FAQ

Common questions

What is inside, how trust works, and how credits are sold.

What is inside a Sunsynk Report?

It moves from an executive overview into data coverage, estimated savings, overall health, energy flows, solar and battery analysis, evening grid dependency, accuracy and fault checks, then prioritised recommendations. You receive a shareable PDF from roughly one month of Sunsynk readings.

Is there a subscription?

No. Bundles are a fixed cost for report credits that never expire. Starter, Classic, and Pro start from £2.99, R69.99, or $4.29 depending on currency. There is no subscription.

Can I trust the data?

Each report includes data coverage and plant details, plus a system accuracy check that verifies energy flows balance within tolerance. Missing or low-quality data is highlighted. Assumptions and limitations are listed in Important Information in the PDF.

Who is the report for?

It is written primarily for Sunsynk system homeowners. Installers may also use the PDF when supporting a homeowner aftercare conversation.

How does report generation work?

Connect your Sunsynk system, analyse about one month of readings, review the executive overview through to recommended actions, then download the PDF. Purchase and generation happen at app.sunsynkreports.com.
