FAQ

Common questions

Clear answers before you buy a report pack.

What do I receive in a Sunsynk Report?

A System Performance Score, six focused reviews, peer benchmarks against neighbouring systems, prioritised actions, and an installer-ready PDF based on roughly one month of Sunsynk readings.

Is there a subscription?

No. Bundles are a fixed cost for report credits that never expire. Starter, Classic, and Pro start from £2.99, R69.99, or $4.29 depending on currency. There is no subscription.

Who is the report for?

Homeowners use it for a plain-language performance snapshot and next actions. Installers and consultants use it for standardised technical documentation and benchmark-backed recommendations.

How does report generation work?

Connect your Sunsynk system, analyse about one month of readings, receive your score and actions, then download the PDF. Purchase and generation happen at app.sunsynkreports.com.
