What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as preferred settings and page interactions.

We use cookies to keep the Sunsynk Report Platform reliable, secure, and easier to use.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for core site functions, such as basic navigation and security-related operations.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with pages and features so we can improve performance and usability over time.

We do not use cookies to sell personal data or build advertising profiles for third-party ad networks.

How Cookies Are Used

  • Maintain and improve website functionality
  • Measure page engagement and journey flow
  • Identify errors and optimise performance
  • Store consent preferences where required

Cookie Duration

Some cookies are session-based and expire when you close your browser. Others are persistent cookies that remain on your device for a defined period to remember settings and preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

Where analytics tools are enabled, third-party providers may set cookies to collect aggregate interaction metrics. These providers process data according to their own privacy notices and contractual terms.

Managing Your Preferences

You can manage cookie choices through the cookie consent banner and your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies, but some platform functionality may be affected.

Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Cookie Policy to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. The latest version will always be published on this page with the update date.
